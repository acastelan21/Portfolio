Let Me Introduce Myself

I am Alvaro Castelan I am a problem solver with the need to be creative. I like being challenged and I have thrived in environments where I had to self teach myself the skills needed for job. I have a track history of being promoted quickly and/or often in my previous jobs. This just goes to show my work ethic and want to do things the right way.

Playing a variety of team sports since I could walk has resulted in me developing many teach skills. I have been on teams where I have had to be a leader and on others where I was a role player. I was able to succeed in both positions. I understand the teams goals come before me and I work to meet and exceed those goals.

I went into the web/app development field because I felt it suited my skills and my personal traits. I have started a full stack coding bootcamp at UT Austin in order to improve my skills. HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, APIs, Node.js, MySql, Express and Firebase are some areas in which I have improved my skills in.
